Basic Tips

Click to see articles with more tips

Use passwords that are 9 characters or longer. Each password should contain at least one uppercase and at least one special character. Avoid passwords containing a whole word. Do not embed personal information in the password.
Design your application with entry validation so that entries cannot execute SQL statements.
Do not open email attachments from senders you do not know. Avoid downloading unverified files from anywhere. Use security tools to check for malware in email attachments.
Avoid entering personal information on public networks, which have no security.
